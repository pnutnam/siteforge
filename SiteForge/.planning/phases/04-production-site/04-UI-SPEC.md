## Typography

| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Body | 16px | 400 (regular) | 1.5 |
| Label | 14px | 500 (medium) | 1.4 |
| Heading | 20px | 600 (semibold) | 1.2 |
| Display | 28px | 600 (semibold) | 1.1 |

**Notes:**
- Heading line-height 1.2 for section titles in Tiptap editor toolbar and accordion headers
- Display line-height 1.1 for page-level headings (hero titles, template picker headings)
- Body line-height 1.5 for readability in paragraph content within Tiptap
- Tiptap editor content renders at body specs; toolbar labels at label specs
