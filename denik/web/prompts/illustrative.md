# Illustrative Design Prompts

Use these prompts to generate illustrative and hand-drawn style variations.

## Variations

### 1. Flat Illustration
Clean, modern flat design aesthetic.
- Solid color fills
- No outlines or subtle shadows
- Minimal detail, maximum impact
- Friendly and approachable

### 2. Line Art
Elegant continuous line drawings.
- Single-weight outlines
- Delicate detailing
- Monochromatic or accent colors
- Sophisticated and refined

### 3. Pop Art
Bold, graphic pop art treatment.
- Halftone dot patterns
- High contrast colors
- Comic-style elements
- Energetic and playful

### 4. Sketch Style
Hand-drawn sketch aesthetic.
- Visible pencil/pen strokes
- Loose, organic lines
- Cross-hatching for shading
- Artistic and authentic

### 5. Retro Illustration
Vintage-inspired illustration styles.
- Art deco elegance
- 1950s Americana
- Vintage travel poster
- Classic advertising aesthetic
