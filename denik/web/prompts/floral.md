# Floral Design Prompts

Use these prompts to generate floral and organic design variations.

## Variations

### 1. Botanical Illustration
Classic botanical style with detailed florals.
- Hand-drawn aesthetic
- Delicate linework with watercolor feel
- Natural color palette
- Elegant and refined

### 2. Modern Floral
Contemporary interpretation with bold florals.
- Large-scale flower motifs
- Vibrant saturated colors
- Abstract flower forms
- Graphic design sensibility

### 3. Watercolor Dreams
Soft, painterly floral compositions.
- Gentle color bleeds
- Soft edges and blending
- Romantic, dreamy atmosphere
- Light background tones

### 4. Tropical Paradise
Exotic florals with tropical flair.
- Bold tropical leaves
- Vibrant sunset colors
- Lush compositions
- SUMMER vibes

### 5. Minimalist Botanical
Clean botanical with modern sensibility.
- Single stem or simple bouquet
- Limited color palette
- Generous white space
- Scandinavian aesthetic
